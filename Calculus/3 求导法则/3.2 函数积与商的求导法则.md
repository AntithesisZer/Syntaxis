# 函数积的求导法则

> [!definition|1] 函数积的求导法则
> 若$f$和$g$都是可微函数，那么
> $$
> \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\left\lbrack f(x)g(x) \right\rbrack = f(x)\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\left\lbrack g(x) \right\rbrack + g(x)\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\left\lbrack f(x) \right\rbrack .
> $$

# 函数商的求导法则

> [!definition|2] 函数商的求导法则
> 若$f$和$g$都是可微函数，那么
> $$
> \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\left\lbrack \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} \right\rbrack = \frac{g(x)\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\left\lbrack f(x) \right\rbrack - f(x)\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\left\lbrack g(x) \right\rbrack}{\left\lbrack g(x) \right\rbrack^{2}} .
> $$
